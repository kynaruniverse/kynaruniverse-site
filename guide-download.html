01. Immediate Retrieval (Email)

Upon successful transaction at the Secure Gateway, our systems automatically dispatch a confirmation signal to your registered email address.

  • Locate the signal from "Kynar Universe".
  • Engage the "Access Protocol" or "Download" button.
  • This link is cryptographically secure and generates a unique instance for your terminal.

02. The Command Center (Account)

If the email signal is interrupted, you may access your permanent digital vault directly from the portal.

  1. Navigate to Command Center via the console header.
  2. Locate the Digital Vault sector.
  3. Click "ACCESS PROTOCOL" next to any synchronized file to initiate retrieval.

03. File Extraction

Most KYNAR assets are compressed into .ZIP archives to ensure data integrity during transmission.

macOS

Double-click the .zip archive. The system will auto-extract the directory.

WINDOWS

Right-click > Select "Extract All" > Designate destination path.
