Common Questions

How do I receive my digital assets?
Immediately after purchase, you'll receive an email from Lemon Squeezy with a secure download link. You can also access your files via the confirmation page.
What software do I need to use these tools?
It depends on the product! Our organization tools typically use Notion, creative templates use Canva, and photo filters work with the free Lightroom Mobile app. Check the product page for specifics.
Do you offer refunds on digital products?
Due to the nature of digital downloads, we generally don't offer refunds once a file has been accessed. However, if you have any technical issues, please contact our support team and we'll make it right.
Can I use these for my business?
Yes! All Kynar Studio assets come with a standard license that allows for personal and commercial use in your own business or social media projects.
